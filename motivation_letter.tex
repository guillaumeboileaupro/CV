Dear Sir or Madam,

I hold a PhD in physics and have more than seven years of experience in data engineering, scientific modeling, and signal processing within complex and international environments. Today, I am seeking to bring my expertise to an innovative, demanding, and ambitious organization such as yours.

Throughout my career, I have developed and integrated advanced analysis and simulation tools for the detection of weak signals and the modeling of instrumental noise. At CNES, I notably designed a modular simulation platform in Python to integrate large-scale models for the LISA space mission (ESA/NASA). \textbf{This pipeline was officially validated by CNES as the foundation for the development of the mission's ground segment, reflecting its robustness, documentation quality, and integration into a long-term scientific data processing architecture.} This experience enabled me to work at the intersection of signal processing challenges, with a team of engineers, system performance, and software architecture.

Previously at the University of Antwerp, I contributed to the development of Bayesian processing chains (MCMC) for identifying and mitigating noise sources in high-precision instruments. I gained strong expertise in statistical inference tools, distributed computing (Condor, Slurm), Linux-based automation, and collaborative development best practices (Git, documentation, continuous integration).

My experience in large international scientific collaborations (LISA, LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA, Einstein Telescope) has taught me to thrive in demanding, collaborative, and technically diverse environments. I played an active role in developing large-scale analysis pipelines, often at the interface between researchers, system engineers, and domain experts. These responsibilities included supervising Master’s students, coordinating critical deliverables, and designing robust solutions for distributed systems. I also led a team of engineers and technicians in an international setting, ensuring technical coordination and delivery of key processing components.

I am proficient with the main tools of the scientific and DevOps ecosystem: Python (pandas, numpy, PyTorch), bash, Condor, Slurm, Docker, Git, SQL, as well as Linux environments and secure protocols (SSH, VPN, data transfer). I am also highly attuned to issues of reproducibility, optimization, and system supervision. I also regularly follow MOOCs to train myself in new tools and keep my technical skills up to date.

Diligent, autonomous, and enthusiastic, I am driven by technological challenges and continuous improvement in data processing systems. I am now looking to join a dynamic team where I can continue designing, automating, and deploying high-impact technical solutions.

Based in the Alpes-Maritimes, I am available from September for a position. I would be very happy to contribute to your projects and bring you my scientific and technical expertise.

Thank you for considering my application. I remain at your disposal for any further discussion.

Yours sincerely,

\textbf{Guillaume Boileau}
